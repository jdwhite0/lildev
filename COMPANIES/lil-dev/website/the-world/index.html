🌍 The Universe

A Neighborhood Built for Discovery

Lil Dev doesn't live in a magical kingdom or a distant galaxy. He lives in a neighborhood — a real, warm, breathing place full of ordinary things that turn extraordinary the moment you start asking questions.

Grandma's kitchen smells like something about to be discovered. The basketball court at night is quiet enough to hear the universe think. The porch at dusk is where the best conversations happen. And somewhere out there, there's a hidden world that only the most curious explorers ever find.

This is the setting. This is the world. And it's always growing.
