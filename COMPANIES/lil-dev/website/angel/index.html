Meet the Companion

Angel — Lil Dev's Dog

Angel has been with Lil Dev through every adventure — the kitchen discoveries, the midnight courts, the hidden worlds. She doesn't always understand everything that's happening. But she's always there. And sometimes, that's exactly what you need.

Angel is more than a pet. She's the emotional center of the universe — the reminder that loyalty and presence are their own kind of superpower.

🐾
Role: Loyal companion and emotional anchor of the LilDev universe.
👀
Superpower: She always knows when something important is about to happen — even before Lil Dev does.
❤️
Bond: Angel and Lil Dev are inseparable — wherever one goes, the other follows.
