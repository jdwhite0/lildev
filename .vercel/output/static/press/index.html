Brand Overview

What Is Lil Dev World?

Lil Dev World is an original children's IP — a growing creative universe built around Lil Dev, a curious, creative kid who turns everyday moments into extraordinary discoveries. Developed by JD Productions, Lil Dev World is designed to grow across stories, animation, games, merchandise, and media.

The franchise launched in May 2026 at lildev.world, with an initial slate of three adventures, two lead characters, and a strategic roadmap that expands the universe across books, browser games, animated content, and a dedicated store at lildev.store.

The core audience is children ages 3–12 and their parents — with content designed to be safe, educational, and genuinely engaging. Every adventure in the Lil Dev universe connects to STEAM themes (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math), but never as a lesson — always as a discovery.

The Characters

The Cast

Lil Dev
Main Character

The curious, creative kid at the center of every adventure. Lil Dev doesn't wait for adventure to find him — he builds it. Everywhere he goes, he asks why. And that question changes everything.

Angel
Companion Character

Lil Dev's loyal dog. Always trotting forward, always alert, always there. Angel doesn't always understand what's happening — but he's always present. That's his superpower.

Adventure Slate

Stories Published (2026)

Kitchen Discovery (Adventure 1) — A beam of morning light sends Lil Dev on a journey of scientific discovery inside his own home. Themes: light, curiosity, asking why. Setting: the kitchen.

Night at the Court (Adventure 2) — Lil Dev returns to an empty basketball court after dark to answer a question only practice can resolve. Themes: perseverance, deliberate practice. Setting: the neighborhood court.

The Hidden World (Adventure 3) — Starting on the porch at dusk, Lil Dev follows a thought from his desk into a discovery about what hides in plain sight. Themes: imagination, discovery, perspective. Setting: porch, desk, discovered space.

The Creator

About JD Productions

JD Productions is an independent creative studio building original IP across storytelling, media, and experience design. Lil Dev World is the studio's flagship children's franchise — built with the intention of growing into a multi-decade, multi-format property.

The studio operates under the belief that children deserve creative universes that respect their intelligence — stories that entertain and enrich without condescending. Lil Dev World is the first major public expression of that philosophy.

For more information about JD Productions, visit jdproductions.io.
